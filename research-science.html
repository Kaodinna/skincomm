Hallmarks of Aging

Healthspan, or "healthy life years," refers to the period of life spent in optimal health, free from major chronic diseases and disabilities. Unlike lifespan, which measures total years lived, healthspan focuses on quality, aiming to extend years of vitality and delay age-related conditions.

Longevity highlights the importance of healthy aging, emphasising that extending lifespan should be paired with improving quality of life by reducing the impact of age-related diseases and disabilities.
