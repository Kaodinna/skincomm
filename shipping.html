Shipping & Delivery

How can I return my purchase?
-

We offer refunds for all products returned within 3 days of purchase, in their unopened and original condition. If you would like to return an item purchased online, please contact us.

Do you provide free shipping within Singapore?
-

Free shipping is available with a minimum order of S$250 per order. For orders below S$250, there will be a delivery fee of S$7 per order. Alternatively, you can opt to self-collect your order at our Experiential Centre.
