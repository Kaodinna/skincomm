Clinically proven & scientifically tested skincare & healthcare brand

SKINCOMM® is a Singapore-based company pioneering Biotechnology for beauty, wellness, and longevity solutions that offers a range of skincare & healthcare products, DNA diagnostic and intervention, and treatment services, featuring the exclusive UNICEL System SkinCeutical collection, UNICEL skin treatments, and the NutraCeutical series.

Our unique selling proposition is grounded in delivering clinically validated, biotechnology-based solutions that integrate beauty, wellness, and longevity into a comprehensive offering.

Our 360-degree Approach

Skincare backed by science and clean formulations to promote healthier and youthful skin.

Certified healthcare supplements powered by gene expression analysis and molecular technology.

DNA diagnostics for personalised health and wellness solutions.

Stem Cell Therapy designed to reverse aging at a cellular level.

We offer an end-to-end solution that no other company currently provides, making us first movers in the holistic, biotechnology-driven wellness market.

Our Team of Experts include prominent doctors, scientists, nutritionists and specialists as our the heart of our company lies a deep commitment to biotechnology-driven innovation. By integrating the latest scientific breakthroughs with consumer needs, we are pioneering a holistic approach to beauty, wellness, anti-aging, and longevity.

Our core strength stems from years of expertise in biotechnology research and development, allowing us to deliver clinically validated, high-performance products and services. This approach not only sets us apart from competitors but positions us as leaders in a fast-growing market that demands both efficacy and safety.
